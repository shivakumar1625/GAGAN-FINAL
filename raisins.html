RAISIN Specifications

Product Name :

RAISIN

Product Description

Our premium quality raisins are sourced from the finest vineyards, ensuring a rich, sweet flavor and a plump, juicy texture. Perfect for snacking, baking, and cooking, our raisins are a versatile addition to any pantry

Varieties

  • Thompson Seedless Raisins
  • Golden Raisins
  • Sultana Raisins
  • Black Raisins

Quality Standards

  • Grade A: Uniform size, excellent color, and flavor, with minimal defects.
  • Grade B: Good quality with slight variations in size and color, maintaining a good flavor.
  • Grade C: Fair quality with more noticeable variations in size and color, suitable for industrial use.
  • Physical Characteristics
  • Moisture Content: ≤ 18% for all varieties except Monukka, which may contain up to 19%.
  • Size: Varies by variety, typically ranging from 0.5 to 1.5 cm in length.
  • Color: Golden, dark brown, or black, depending on the variety.

Nutritional Information (per 100g)

Calories: 299 kcal Carbohydrates: 79g
Sugars: 59g
Dietary Fiber: 4g
Protein: 3g
Fat: 0.5g

Packaging

Bulk Packaging: 10kg, 20kg, and 25kg cartons.
Retail Packaging: 250g, 500g, and 1kg resealable bags.

Certifications

  • ISO 22000: Food Safety Management
    HACCP: Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points
    Organic Certification: Available upon request

Storage and Shelf Life

  • Storage Conditions: Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
    Shelf Life: Up to 12 months under proper storage conditions./li>

Sustainability Practices

  • Eco-Friendly Farming: Our raisins are grown using sustainable farming practices that minimize environmental impact.
    Fair Trade: We ensure fair wages and working conditions for all our farmers and workers.
